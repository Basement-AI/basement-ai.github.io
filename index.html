Aarne Talman

Language Technology Researcher

I'm a computational linguist and language technology researcher working on language understanding and reasoning.

I work as a Lead AI Scientist at Silo AI. I'm also a Doctoral Researcher and Visiting Scholar in Language Technology at University of Helsinki. I have almost 20 years of experience in research, software engineering, consulting and leadership.

My research focuses on natural language understanding, reasoning and natural language inference using machine learning. I'm interested in what understanding language consists of, how it can be modelled in AI and how these models can be evaluated.

I hold an MSc in Computational Linguistics and Formal Grammar from King's College London (2007) and a BSc in Philosophy from London School fo Economics (2005).
