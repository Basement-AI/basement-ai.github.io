About Basement AI

Basement AI focuses on helping organisations to identify and unlock the value of their data through artificial intelligence. We conduct research on natural language understanding and support both commercial and public sector organizations with their data, AI and natural language processing related initiatives. Our expertise and experience ranges from strategy definition to hands-on development of state-of-the-art machine learning and NLP systems.

Aarne Talman

Founder

       

Aarne has 15 years of experience in AI, ranging from software development and academic research to strategy consulting. Prior to founding Basement AI, Aarne led the Analytics consulting practice in the Nordic region at Gartner. He has also worked as the UK CTO and Global Machine Learning Practice Lead at Nordcloud. He holds a PhD research position in Language Technology at University of Helsinki.
